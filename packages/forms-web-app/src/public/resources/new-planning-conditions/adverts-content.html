The standard conditions

The development must:

  • Do not display an advertisement without the landowner’s (or someone with a legal interest’s) permission.
  • Do not display an advertisement in a way that:
    • puts people at risk on roads, railways, waterways, docks, harbours or aerodromes (civil or military)
    • blocks or makes it harder to understand traffic signs, railway signals, or navigation aids for water or air traffic
    • interferes with security or surveillance equipment, or devices used to measure vehicle speed
  • Keep the advertisement and the land or buildings that you use to display them in good condition and do not harm the appearance of the area.
  • Keep any structure or hoarding that you use to display advertisements safe and do not put the public at risk.
  • If you need to remove an advertisement, leave the site safe and tidy. Do not harm public safety or the appearance of the area.
