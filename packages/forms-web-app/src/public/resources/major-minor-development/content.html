A major development is for any application that includes:

  • developing 10 dwellings or more
  • a site area over 0.5 hectares (when you do not know the number of dwellings)
  • a floor space of over 1,000 square metres or a site area of one hectare
  • mineral extraction
  • waste development

A minor development is for any application that includes:

  • developing an existing dwelling or within the land around it
  • extending an existing building for non-domestic use (up to 250 square metres of floor space)
  • altering an existing building without increasing its size
